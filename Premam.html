Premam (transl.â€‰Love) is a 2015 Indian Malayalam coming of age romance film written, directed and edited by Alphonse Puthren. Produced by Anwar Rasheed for Anwar Rasheed Entertainment, the film stars Nivin Pauly, Sai Pallavi, Madonna Sebastian, Anupama Parameswaran, Shabareesh Varma, Krishna Sankar, Ananth Nag, Siju Wilson and others in the supporting roles and featured 18 debutant actors. Rajesh Murugesan composed the music and Anand C. Chandran handled the film's cinematography. The plot follows George and his friends from their teenage to adulthood.

The film was included in The Hindu's top 25 Malayalam films of the decade and eventually attained a cult status.[7] The film garnered awards and nominations in several categories, with particular praise for its direction, screenplay, Nivin and Pallavi's performance, music, cinematography, and editing.[8][9] The film had a theatrical run of 175 days in Kerala and 275 days in Chennai, Tamil Nadu.[10][11] The film was also remade in Telugu in 2016 with the same name.
